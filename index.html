08 Straight answers

The questions you're actually asking.

Do I need to know how to code? +
No. Frai is built for business owners and operators, not engineers. You'll work in friendly tools with step-by-step guidance, and Lawal is in the build with you. By the end you'll understand your system well enough to extend it, and that's the point.
What exactly do I leave with? +
A deployed AI tool running on your own business data (a RAG chatbot, document assistant, or support agent) that your customers or team can actually use. Plus the knowledge to maintain and grow it.
Will I also learn to use ChatGPT and Claude? +
Yes. Beyond the tool you build, you'll learn to properly use everyday AI like ChatGPT and Claude: how to prompt, instruct, and direct them to draft, analyse, research, and make decisions. That skill stays with you and speeds up your work long after the cohort ends.
Why only 30 seats? +
Because this is built with you, not broadcast at you. A small cohort means real attention on your specific build during office hours. When the 30 are claimed, Cohort 01 closes and the next intake is ₦150k+.
What does ₦100k cover? +
The full 4-week live program, the build sessions, private office hours, and the alumni group. It's the founding-cohort price, locked only for Cohort 01. Hiring a developer to build the same tool typically runs ₦300k–500k+, and you wouldn't keep the skill.
Is the waitlist a payment? +
No. Joining the waitlist or applying costs nothing. Accepted applicants get a private 48-hour window to claim a seat at ₦100k before seats open to the public.
Remote or in person? +
Cohort 01 runs live and remote-friendly so founders across Nigeria can join, with Lagos-based sessions where it helps. Details go out to accepted applicants.
